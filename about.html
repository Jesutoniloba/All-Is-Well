About The Redeemed Christian Church of God

The Redeemed Christian Church of God (RCCG) is a worldwide church organization founded in 1952 with the vision of bringing people into a deep relationship with God through Jesus Christ.

Our Vision

To make heaven. To take as many people with us. To have a member of RCCG in every family of all nations. To accomplish this by holiness, and by planting churches within five minutes walking distance in every city and town.

Our Mission

To make heaven and take as many people as possible with us by living a life of holiness and evangelism. To proclaim the good news of Jesus Christ to every nation and disciple souls for the Kingdom of God.

What We Believe

  • The Bible is the inspired Word of God.
  • There is only one God, eternally existent in three persons: Father, Son, and Holy Spirit.
  • Jesus Christ is the Son of God, who died for our sins and rose again.
  • Salvation is available to all who repent and believe in Christ.
  • We believe in the power of the Holy Spirit, divine healing, and holy living.
