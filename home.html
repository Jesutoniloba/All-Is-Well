ABOUT

Who We Are

At RCCG All is Well Parish, we are a community built on faith, prayer, and love. Our mission is to raise a generation of believers who live in righteousness, demonstrate God’s power, and make a lasting impact in their families, communities, and the world. We believe that with Christ, truly all is well.

OUR UNITS

Departments

All is Well Parish has various departments that support the smooth running of the church from leadership to choir, ushering, and sanitation. We believe every department is equally important in fulfilling God’s purpose.
